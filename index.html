About me

I’m Eya Adouni, a 21-year-old Full-Stack Developer from Gabès, Tunisia, specialized in MERN, PHP, Laravel, JavaScript, and MySQL. With a strong background in both web and mobile development, I also focus on system maintenance, performance optimization, and clean design.

A graduate in Information Systems Development, currently pursuing a Master’s in Telecommunications, I work as an independent developer and researcher. I build functional, user-centered applications and websites — from concept to deployment — while ensuring smooth performance, maintenance, and UI/UX precision.

As a passionate freelancer, I design and deliver digital solutions, solve complex technical issues, and bring innovation into every line of code. I’ve explored over 6 research articles in fields like IoT, big data, and cloud computing, and turned them into working projects.

Outside of tech, I run my own handmade bag brand, where creativity meets entrepreneurship. I'm also deeply fascinated by astronomy — a curiosity that has inspired me since childhood and continues to shape the way I approach science and technology.
